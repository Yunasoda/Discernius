Ancient Origins

The history of Discernius stretches back millennia, to a time when primitive star-faring civilizations first made contact with one another. While the early records of this period are fragmented, it is believed that ancient cultures across multiple star systems developed independently, until the advent of faster-than-light travel allowed for widespread interaction and trade between the systems.

Age of Expansion

The Age of Expansion, beginning around 3,000 years ago, was a time of rapid colonization and exploration. Technological advancements led to the establishment of massive interstellar empires, each seeking to expand their influence across the galaxy. Many of these empires still exist in some form today, their legacy shaping the current political and social structure of Discernius.

The Great Wars

The Great Wars of Discernius were a series of devastating conflicts that spanned centuries. Entire civilizations were brought to the brink of destruction, and many were lost to the ravages of time. These wars reshaped the galaxy, leading to the fall of once-mighty empires and the rise of new powers.

Modern Era

In the Modern Era, Discernius is a galaxy in constant flux. The political landscape remains fractured, with numerous factions vying for control. Advances in both technology and magic have led to new forms of warfare, as well as new alliances and rivalries. Though the galaxy has seen moments of peace, the shadow of conflict always looms.
