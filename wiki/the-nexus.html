The Nexus

Organization Information

The official emblem of the Discernius Syndicate.

Founded: 563 DS

Leader(s): Tashnav Lorica

Type: Paramilitary Syndicate

Headquarters: TaNexia

Motto: “Harmony in dominance.”

Overview

The Nexus is a shadowy and powerful organization in the Discernius Galaxy. Operating behind the scenes, the Nexus manipulates galactic events through covert actions and strategic alliances. Its primary focus is on securing and controlling the galaxy's remaining magical resources, as well as exerting its influence on various political factions. The Nexus is ruled by an enigmatic Shadow Council, whose members are rumored to be among the most powerful beings in the galaxy, blending both magic and technology to maintain their dominance.

Operations and Influence

The Nexus operates in a galaxy fragmented by political chaos, where no singular power holds complete control. Through a web of infiltration, subterfuge, and assassination, the Nexus ensures its survival and growth. Its operatives often remain unseen, and even their presence is usually unknown until a mission has already been executed.

While the Nexus uses technology to monitor and influence individuals — such as cybernetic chips implanted at birth — it also wields magic, making it unique in the Discernius Galaxy. The organization primarily seeks out and secures sources of magical energy, which are dwindling as the galaxy's resources are stretched thin. This magic is most commonly found within the leadership of monarchies or the upper echelons of powerful political bodies, and the Nexus is constantly vying to control these rare and potent sources.

Nexus Divisions

The Nexus is divided into two main operational arms: the Tactical Infiltration Division (TI) and the Enforcement Division (ENF). Each of these divisions plays a critical role in the Nexus' operations across the galaxy.

I. Tactical Infiltration Division (TI)

The Tactical Infiltration Division handles the covert operations of the Nexus, specializing in espionage, sabotage, and strategic assassinations. Its operatives are some of the most elite in the galaxy, often enhanced by cybernetic modifications and possessing magical abilities. TI operatives work in the shadows to destabilize governments, remove key figures, and manipulate the outcomes of galactic events to the Nexus' favor.

II. Enforcement Division (ENF)

The Enforcement Division functions as the military arm of the Nexus. Where Tactical Infiltration uses stealth, the ENF employs brute force. The Enforcement battalions are deployed to enforce the will of the Nexus in key sectors, often acting as a blunt instrument to crush rebellion and secure strategic locations. These operations are usually direct, leaving no room for negotiation, only submission.
