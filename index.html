What is Discernius?

Discernius is a vast, dystopian galaxy where technology dominates and ancient forces linger. The atmosphere is dark and oppressive, with fractured political systems, constant power struggles, and a sense of deep control over its inhabitants. Amid this, mysterious elements of magic exist, though rarely understood.

The world of Discernius is defined by its complexity—where survival requires navigating a realm shaped by ambition, secrecy, and the uneasy balance between progress and ancient power.
